R.M.K Green Policy

  • RMKEC strives to be a leader among the all the educational institutes in achieving the environmental excellence.
  • We are committed to develop green thinking and green practices into all aspects of daily operations.
  • We aim to reduce waste production by recycling and reusing.
  • We envision a workplace where every process has been “greened”.
  • We aim to establish and follow principles, in conjunction with environmental practices.
  • We aim for moving towards renewable energy from conventional energy.
